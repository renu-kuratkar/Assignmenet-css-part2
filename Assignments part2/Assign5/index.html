Sare Jahan Se Acha Lyrics

Sare jahan se accha hindostan hamara Hum bulbulain hai iss ki yeh gulsitan hamara ||Sare || Ghurbat men hon agar ham rahta hai dil vatan men Samjho vahin hamen bhi dil hain jahan hamara||Sare || Parbat voh sab se uncha hamsaya asman ka Voh santari hamara voh pasban hamara||Sare ||

Godi men khelti hain is ki hazaron nadiya Gulshan hai jin ke dam se rashk-e-janan hamara||Sare || Aye ab raud ganga voh din hen yad tujhko Utara tere kinare jab karvan hamara ||Sare || Mazhab nahin sikhata apas men bayr rakhna Hindvi hai ham vatan hai hindostan hamara||Sare ||

Yunan o misr o roma sab mil gaye jahan se Ab tak magar hai baqi nam o nishan hamara||Sare || Kuch bat hai keh hasti milati nahin hamari Sadiyon raha hai dushman daur e zaman hamara||Sare || Iqbal koi meharam apna nahin jahan men Malum kya kisi ko dard e nihan hamara||Sare ||
